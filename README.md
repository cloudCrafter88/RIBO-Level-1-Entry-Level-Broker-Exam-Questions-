## What Is the RIBO Level 1 Exam?

The RIBO Level 1 exam is a three-hour multiple-choice licensing test administered by the Registered Insurance Brokers of Ontario (RIBO).

Passing this exam qualifies candidates to begin working as entry-level brokers in Ontario. Successful candidates may also sell Travel Health Insurance, subject to licensing approval.

## Key Topics to Focus On

To pass the RIBO Level 1 Exam, candidates should thoroughly study:

- The RIB Act, Regulations, and RIBO By-laws  
- Insurance ethics and brokerage responsibilities  
- Automobile insurance coverage and policy structure  
- Habitational insurance (homeowners, tenants, condominium)  
- Travel health insurance basics  
- Commercial insurance fundamentals  
- Industry standards and compliance requirements  

Many questions are scenario-based, so understanding coverage details, exclusions, and broker duties is essential.

## Licensing Eligibility Requirements

Before registering for the exam, ensure you meet the following criteria:

- Minimum 18 years of age  
- Valid criminal record history  
- Compliance with the Only Business or Employment rule  

Prospective candidates should review RIBO’s licensing qualification criteria before attempting the exam.
